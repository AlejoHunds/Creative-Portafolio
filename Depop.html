Depop Visual Concept

Exploring digital fashion marketplaces and youth culture.

Concept

This project explores the visual language of online fashion marketplaces through the lens of Depop. The concept focuses on community-driven commerce, personal branding and the aesthetic codes of second-hand fashion culture.

Design Direction

The design direction highlights authenticity and individuality. Layout decisions prioritise large imagery, minimal typography and clean spacing to reflect the curated and visual nature of fashion resale platforms.
